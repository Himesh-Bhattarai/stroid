# Praise

This book intentionally does not include endorsement quotes.

That is not because praise pages are bad.
It is because fake praise is worse than no praise.

At this stage:

- no external endorsement has been formally collected
- no expert quote has been approved for publication
- no testimonial is being invented just to make the book look finished

If a future edition includes endorsements, they should come from:

- people who actually read the documentation
- people who actually used the library
- people willing to make specific claims, not vague compliments

Until then, this section stays honest by staying plain.
